Our Mission

Pose Click Shoot is a freelance photography company that
specializes in portraits and editorial photos. It is a self-
starter company that has accomplished many achievements
in the 3 years it has been running for. It is based in Los
Angeles with CEO Noor Shami running the business. Clients
can reach out with any creative vision in mind and the
company can come up with a guide to exceed their
expectations.
